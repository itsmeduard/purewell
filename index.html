Cardiology

Focuses on diagnosing, treating, and preventing heart and vascular diseases, addressing conditions like hypertension, coronary artery disease, and heart failure. Cardiologists use advanced diagnostic tools and treatments to improve heart health, enhance quality of life, and reduce the risk of cardiovascular events.

Neurology

Medicine dedicated to diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. Neurologists manage conditions like epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, and migraines, using advanced techniques to improve patient outcomes and quality of life

Hepatology

Specialized field of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of liver diseases, including hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Hepatologists use advanced diagnostic methods and therapeutic approaches to manage liver conditions and improve overall liver health.

Pediatrics

Medicine dedicated to the health and medical care of infants, children, and adolescents. Pediatricians diagnose and treat a wide range of childhood conditions, from common illnesses and developmental disorders to complex medical conditions, ensuring comprehensive care tailored to the needs of young patients.

Eye Care

Diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of visual and ocular disorders, including common issues like nearsightedness and glaucoma, as well as complex conditions such as retinal diseases. Eye care professionals, including optometrists and ophthalmologists, use advanced techniques and technologies to maintain and improve vision health.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions.

What services does PureWell offer?

PureWell offers a comprehensive range of healthcare services, including preventive care, general check-ups, chronic disease management, and specialized treatments. Our team of experienced medical professionals is dedicated to providing personalized care tailored to your specific needs.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. However, it's essential to verify your coverage with your insurance provider to understand your benefits and any applicable co-pays or deductibles.

What are your clinic hours?

Our clinic is open from 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM Monday to Saturday. We offer flexible appointment scheduling options to accommodate your busy lifestyle. You can book an appointment online or by calling our clinic directly.

Do you offer telemedicine or online consultations?

Yes, we offer telemedicine services for the convenience of our patients. You can consult with our doctors remotely through secure video conferencing. Please contact our clinic to schedule a telemedicine appointment.

What is your appointment scheduling process?

Scheduling an appointment at PureWell is easy. You can book an appointment online through our website or by calling our clinic directly. Our friendly staff will assist you in finding a suitable appointment time that fits your schedule.
